Who is OT?

OT Design was founded in 2019 by Olivia Ruby Tucker. As a senior Media Arts and Design Student, Art and Photography have inspired OT Design to combine digital media platforms and user experience to create a design firm with passion and purpose.

What is OT Design?

OT Design is a portfolio website that is intended to house all of my various projects and work. I created this site in order to showcase some of my favorite graphic, UX/UI, and fine art pieces.

Why OTD?

Through OTD, I am hoping to inspire others and grown my own personal brand. OTD is the perfect outlet where I can practice my coding and web design skills while adding to my portfolio. Eventually, I want to bring OTD to life as a multimedia creative service provider.
